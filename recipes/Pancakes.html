    Steps
  1. Mash banana in a bowl using a fork
  2. add eggs, baking powder, and cinnamon and mix batter well
  3. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat
  4. Spoon batter into the hot butter and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 2 to 3 minutes
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter
